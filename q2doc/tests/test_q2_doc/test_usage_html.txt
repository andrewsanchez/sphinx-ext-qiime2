<p class="admonition-title">Note</p>
<p>the <cite>forward.fastq.gz</cite> and <cite>metadata.tsv</cite> files will be stored under
the <cite>data/</cite> dir, which will be the extent of the data scope. Everything
stored in <cite>data/</cite> will get download blocks generated automatically.</p>
